The Best Movies According to Khristen

My top 3 movies of all time.

Alice in Wonderland

This has been my favorite movie for as long
as I can remember. I love the thought of being
lost in Wonderland.

Sucker Punch

This movie gives me chills, the soundtrack
is amazing, the visuals are insane. It is gritty and
beautiful at the same time.

Spirited Away

This was my first introduction to Studeio Gihbli
and has sent me down a path of loving all things
Japanese.
